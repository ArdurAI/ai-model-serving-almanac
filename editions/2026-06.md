## Model Serving — vLLM/SGLang/TensorRT-LLM Triad

### The state of the category

Three dominant open-source engines: [vLLM](../tools/vllm.md) (broadest support), [SGLang](../tools/sglang.md) (RAG/multi-turn leader), [TensorRT-LLM](../tools/tensorrt-llm.md) (highest throughput on NVIDIA). [NVIDIA Dynamo](../tools/nvidia-dynamo.md) 1.0 (Mar 2026) is the production-grade distributed orchestration layer, replacing Triton for LLMs. K8s-native serving is the production default.

### Findings
- **TGI entered maintenance mode** as of late 2025; new optimizations land in vLLM and SGLang first.
- **Ollama is not production-ready** for multi-tenant: collapses under 5 concurrent users in benchmarks.
- **Specialized AI hardware**: Groq acquired by NVIDIA ($20B), Cerebras filed for IPO ($1B+ revenue), SambaNova acquired by Intel ($1.6B).
- **Edge/on-device now production-ready**: ExecuTorch (Meta, 1.0 GA Oct 2025, 50KB footprint); MLX (Apple, 24.6K+ stars, 4,255+ HF models).
